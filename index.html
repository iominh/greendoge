Cannonball

So energy. Much saving.

Cannonball is a social game that lets players compete in the green race to save energy. It is named after the Cannonball Run, a cross-country race in which drivers test their mettle in a drive from New York to San Francisco as fast as they can.

Here, players use the energy saved compared to state and regional averages to power their chosen vehicles across the nation. Can you beat your friends?
